garbage in bodies of water affect the plants and creatures living below the water?

Well, from what I have concluded, plastic debris and other garbage in the

oceans cause plants and animals to either perish or get diseases, for

the most part. So if you're carrying any sort of garbage, I recommend you

take the extra mile to hold on to that piece of garbage, and wait until you

can properly dispose it.

What causes ocean pollution?

Pollution in the ocean comes from a variety of sources, such as agricultural fertilizers and pesticides as well as marine litter. Marine litter is human-created waste that reaches coastal or marine environments.

How can we prevent ocean pollution due to plastics?

We need to improve the systems that collect, sort and treat waste components. Current composting, recycling and recovery programs help prevent plastics from reaching the oceans, but there is much need for improvement.

How many marine animals are affected by debris in the ocean per year?

Over 1 million marine animals are killed each year due to plastic debris in the oceans.

89% of plastic litter found on the ocean floor are single-use items like plastic bags

Every day, approximately 8 million pieces of plastic, find their way into our oceans

Around 13 million tonnes of plastic reach the ocean each year
